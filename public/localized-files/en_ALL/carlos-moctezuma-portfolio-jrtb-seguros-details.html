JRTB Seguros Website

Project information

  • Category: Web design
  • Client: JRTB Seguros
  • Project date: 12 December, 2019
  • Project URL: jrtbseguros.mx

JRTB Seguros needed an informative website that complies with the regulations of the National Commission of Insurance and Bonds (CNFS) for its acronym in Spanish, besides being able to inform, the client needed to be able to collect information necessary to quote different products, for this we created 4 different "quotes" forms that generate the relevant information for each type of quote, also the site complies with personal data protection policies GDPR, CCPA and the current Mexican regulation.
